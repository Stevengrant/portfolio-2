About Me

Searching for new directions, I lived in this farcical, 1993 SF life. I'm an ex-cop freed from 36 years of forced hibernation to help catch a criminal who released himself from a similar incarceration. In my futuristic story I find Los Angeles a sea of Taco Bells and enforced peace, and within that satiric overview I became a gun-toting fish out of water. My life plays like a live-action cartoon, and while there is nothing particularly wrong with that, my life's a rather flat experience. The irony of a peaceable society that both requires and despises its bloody saviors has been captured far more profoundly in movies like Dirty Harry.
